What do you need help with?

    How to login?

    While you are on the login page:

  1. Simply add your username and password with which you registered with and click on the blue 'login' button.
  2. If you have forgotton your password, click on the 'forgot password?' link and follow the instructons.
  3. If you do not have an account, click on the 'Dont have an account' link and register for an account using your chosen details.

    How to Register?

    To register for an account the following information will be required:

    • Username
    • Email Adress
    • Password
    • Confirmation of password
  1. Please enter the required information specified above in the relevant fields.
  2. Click on the blue 'Register' button

    3. If you already have an account, click on the 'Already have an account?' link and login with your registered details.

    How to add a Domain

    To add a Domain expand the sidebar and while the sidebar is expanded click on the 'Add a Domain' button.

  1. The add Domain modal will appear, enter a Domain name of your choice in the field.
  2. A description of your chosen domain.
  3. A chosen image for your domain.

    4. Domains will act as a 'folder' for your sources, so you can group spurces together about a specific domain.

    How to remove a Domain

  1. To remove a domain expand the sidebar and while the sidebar is expanded, click on the 'bin' icon and this will cause a pop-up moda to appear.
  2. The removal of the specified Domain will will occur after confirmation from the user.

    How to edit a Domain

    To edit a Domain, expand the sidebar and while it is expanded:

  1. Click on the 'edit' button which is the pencil icon
  2. This will allow you to edit the name
  3. This will allow you to change the description
  4. Will allow you to change the image of the specified domain

    How to add a Source

    Navigate to the select source bar at the top of the page

  1. Click on the blue '+' button to begin adding a Source.
  2. Add the source name.
  3. Choose between the tested Sources(Google reviews,Trip-Adviser and Youtube).
  4. Add a source URL, for Youtube add a link to a Youtube video, for Trip advisor add a link to a Trip advisor Domain and for Google reviews add a link to a Google maps location.
  5. Click confirm to save the Source.

    How to remove a Source

    Navigate to the select source bar at the top of the page

  1. To remove a Source, click on the source you would like to remove and click on the red 'bin' button.
  2. The Source will be removed after confirmation from the user.

    How to refresh a Source

    Navigate to the select source bar at the top of the page

  1. To refresh the data of a source, click on the green refresh button on the right hand side of the page.

    How to edit a Source

    To edit a Source, click on the source you would like to edit and click on the yellow edit button on the right hand side of the page and an edit source modal will appear.

  1. Edit the name of the source.
  2. Edit the source URL of a chosen source.
  3. Once you have edited the relevant information, click on the blue 'confirm' button to save the changes.

    How to toggle theme

    To toggle a theme, click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar and a profile modal will appear.

  1. Click on the 'Toggle Theme' button to toggle between a light and dark theme.

    How to log out

  1. To log out click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar and a profile modal will appear, click on the 'Log Out' button to log out of the app.

    How to delete account

    To delete your account click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar and a profile modal will appear.

  1. Click on the 'Delete Account' button.
  2. Confirm that you do want to delete your account.
  3. Enter your password to confirm account deletion.

    How to update profile picture

    To update your profile picture click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar and a profile modal will appear.

  1. Click on the profile picture itself to update your profile picture and then click the 'Confirm' button to save the changes.

    Explanation of center console

  1. Overall score is the ratio of positive and negative sentiment of a chosen domain, the higher the score the more positive the sentiment data and vice versa.
  2. The amount of positive, negative and neutral sentiment of a chosen domain which are represented in percentages, the higher the percentage the more that sentiment is present in the chosen domain.
  3. The Emotion block of the center console is the most prevelant emotion of a chosen domain, the emotions detectable are anger, disgust, fear, joy, neutral, sadness and finally surprise.
  4. The toxicity block aims to access the level of harmfulness or offensiveness in the text and classify it accordingly.
  5. The analysed block presents the amount of sentiment that has been analysed in a chosen domain.
  6. The Range block is the timeframe in which the data has been collected to calculate the statistics and insights.
