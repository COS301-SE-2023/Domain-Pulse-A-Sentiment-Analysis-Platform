Help Page

Account Creation

    How to login?

    While you are on the login page:

  1. Simply add your username and password with which you registered with and click on the blue 'login' button.
  2. If you have forgotten your password, click on the 'forgot password?' link and follow the instructions.
  3. If you do not have an account, click on the 'Don't have an account' link and register for an account using your chosen details.

    How to Register?

    To register for an account the following information will be required:

    • Username
    • Email Address
    • Password
    • Confirmation of password
  1. Please enter the required information specified above in the relevant fields.
  2. Click on the blue 'Register' button

    3. If you already have an account, click on the 'Already have an account?' link and login with your registered details.

Domain Management

    How to add a Domain

    To add a domain, navigate to the sidebar and click on the 'Add a Domain' button.

  1. The add domain modal will appear, enter a domain name of your choice in the text field.
  2. Add a description for your domain.
  3. Choose an image for your domain

    4. Domains will act as a 'folder' for your sources, so you can group sources together for a specific domain.

    How to remove a Domain

  1. To remove a domain expand the sidebar (by clicking on the expand button on the top of the sidebar), click on the 'bin' icon.
  2. Confirm that you do want to delete the domain

    How to edit a Domain

    To edit a Domain, expand the sidebar.

  1. Click on the 'pencil' button to start editing
  2. Edit the name of the domain
  3. Edit the description of the domain
  4. Change the image of the domain

Source Management

    How to add a Source

    Navigate to the source-selector bar at the top of the dashboard

  1. Click on the blue '+' button to begin adding a Source.
  2. Add the source name.
  3. Select a source type
  4. Add an appropriate URL for your chosen source
  5. Click confirm to save the Source.

    How to delete a Source

    Navigate to the source-selector bar at the top of the dashboard

  1. To delete a Source, click on the source you would like to delete and click on the red 'bin' button on the right.
  2. Confirm that you want to delete/remove the source

    How to refresh a Source

    Navigate to the source-selector bar at the top of the dashboard

  1. To refresh a source's data, click on the green refresh button on the right hand side of the dashboard.

    How to edit a Source

    To edit a Source, click on the source you would like to edit and click on the 'info' icon that is on your source's card.

  1. Edit the name of the source.
  2. Edit the source URL of a chosen source.
  3. Once you have edited the relevant information, click on the blue 'confirm' button to save the changes.

Profile Management

    How to toggle theme

    To toggle a theme, click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar to access the profile section.

  1. Click on the 'Toggle Theme' button to toggle between a light and dark theme.

    How to log out

  1. To log out click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar to access the profile section, click on the 'Log Out' button to log out of the app.

    How to delete account

    To delete your account click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar to access the profile section.

  1. Click on the 'Delete Account' button.
  2. Enter your password to confirm account deletion.

    How to update profile picture

    To update your profile picture click on the profile icon at the bottom of the sidebar to access the profile section.

  1. Click on the profile picture itself to update your profile picture, upload a new picture, and then click the 'Confirm' button to save the changes.

Dashboard Configuration

    Explanation of center console

  1. Overall score is a metric calculated from numerous statistics. The higher the score the more positive the sentiment data and vice versa.
  2. The amount of positive, negative and neutral sentiment. The higher the percentage the more that sentiment is present in the chosen domain.
  3. The Emotion statistic is the most prevalent emotion of a chosen domain, the emotions detectable are anger, disgust, fear, joy, neutral, sadness and finally surprise.
  4. The toxicity statistic represents the level of harmfulness or offensiveness in the text and classifies it accordingly.
  5. The analysed statistic shows the number of sentiments that have been analysed.
  6. The range statistic is the timeframe over which the data has been collected.

    Explanation of Graphs and charts console

  1. The graphs shown are graphical representations of the statistics and insights shown in the center console of the page.
  2. Clicking on the arrows on the left and right of the graphs will allow you to view the graphs of the previous and next statistic respectively. Additionally, you can naviagte the graphs by clicking on the dots on the bottom of the graph.
  3. To view the statistics for different graphs, click on different statiistics in the statistics center console.

    This is where you can view sample data (reviews/comments) of a chosen domain.

  1. The first metric is the overall score statistic.
  2. The second metric is the overall categorisation of the sentiment.
  3. The most prevalent emotion found within review.
  4. A classification of the toxicity of the review.

Source Creation

    How to find a Youtube video URL

  1. Navigate to a browser of your choice.
  2. Navigate to Youtube.
  3. Search for a video of your choice that you would want to be used as a source.
  4. Copy the URL from the search bar.
  5. Paste the URL into the URL field of the add source modal.

    How to find a Google reviews URL

  1. Navigate to a browser of your choice.
  2. Navigate to Google Maps.
  3. Search for a location of your choice that you would like to use as a source.
  4. Click on the share button and copy the URL (link)
  5. Paste the URL into the URL field of the add source menu.

    How to find a Trip-Advisor URL

  1. Navigate to a browser of your choice.
  2. Navigate to Trip-Advisor.
  3. Search for a location of your choice that you would like to use as a source.
  4. Copy the URL from the search bar at the top of the page.
  5. Paste the URL into the URL field of the add source modal.

    How to find a TrustPilot URL

  1. Navigate to a browser of your choice.
  2. Navigate to Trust Pilot.
  3. Search for a company or category of your choice that you would like to use as a source.
  4. Copy the URL from the search bar at the top of the page.
  5. Paste the URL into the URL field of the add source modal.

    How to add a csv as a source

  1. The csv file has to follow the specific format in which the example file shows below:
    • Example csv file
    • The columns must have the same headings as the example file and the date format must be the same.
    • Ensure that your review test is contained within quotation marks ("")
    • If you have followed the above guidelines, but it still does not work, ensure that you're .csv is encoded using UTF-8
  2. To add a csv file as a source , navigate to the add source menu.
  3. Add your csv file to use it as a source.

    How do live reviews work?

    Live review is as an anonymous review platform that analyses reviews from people in real time.

  1. Create a live reviews source.
  2. Navigate to the information regarding the live source.
  3. Share the link or QR code.
  4. The availability of the link can be changed to the preference of the user who started the live review source.
  5. Start collecting live data!
  6. Whenever you want to see new data, hit the refresh button in the top right corner

Other

    How do I reset my password?

  1. If you forgot your password, please contact support at domainpulse.help@gmail.com

    What to do if the website is not responding?

  1. Try to refresh the website.
  2. If the above step does not help then try clearing cookies and refreshing the page after cookies have been cleared.
  3. Make sure that your internet connection is stable and that you are indeed connected to the internet.

    Remove unwanted reviews from source

  1. If you have comments/reviews that you would like to remove for any of the following reasons:
    • The review does not hold any importance/relevance to your source
    • The review was made with malicious purposes
    • The review is completely obscene or contains hate speech
  2. Contact domainpulse.help@gmail.com with the name of your domain, the name of your source and the comment text.
