Domain Pulse is the ultimate sentiment analysis platform. It gathers and analyses online opinions about any domain, be it a business, a person, or more. With stunning visuals and easy-to-understand statistics, Domain Pulse helps you understand the online presence and sentiment for any domain.

Our Services

What can Domain pulse be used for?

Product Research

Domain pulse can analyse the sentiment regarding a product and give you insightful statistics to aid in decision making around purchasing products.

Customer feedback analysis

Customer feedback can be ran through Domain pulse and the application will show you everything you need to know about how your customer base is feeling!

Market trend analysis

Investors can use Domain pulse to determine the overall feeling regarding certain equities / stocks and use this information to aid in investing.

Competitor analysis

Domain pulse can be used to analyse competitors products and services, this information can aid in making important business decisions.

Product launch assessment

After a new product is launched by a business, Domain pulse can be used to determine the sentiment regarding the newly released product.

Steps to use Domain Pulse

1

 

Add a Domain and source of your choice

Add a domain and source of your choice to the Domain pulse platform. The platform will then gather data from the source and analyse it.

2

 

View the statistics and insights

Wait until the application has generated all necessary statistics and insights. You will then be able to view these statistics and insights on the dashboard.

3

 

Generate a report

Generate a report based on the statistics and insights generated by the application. This report can be used to make informed decisions regarding certain topics.

4

 

Repeat for any Domain you wish

Repeat the process for any domain you wish to analyse. The application can be used to analyse any domain, be it a business, a person, or more.
